Who am I?

If you asked yourself this question, or if you want to know me better not just as a web developer and designer, but also as a person, you are in the right place.

And another pic of myself is there again. Plus, some info about me.

So, first things first, my full name is Daniela Vornic, I was born in the Republic of Moldova and I’m 14. My native language is Romanian, but I also can speak English very well.

If speaking about my niche, I began my journey with Adobe Photoshop. Then I remember researching about graphic design. From there, I started to learn the HTML & CSS, and after a time learning and practicing here I am.

When I’m not designing or coding, I usually spend my time reading, riding my bike if I have access to it or playing badminton, drawing, planning/dreaming and debating ideas, or just relaxing. I go to school, too.

That’s what I can say about me, the important stuff!
